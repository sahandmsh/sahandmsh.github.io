About Me

I am an AI/ML software engineer with hands-on experience building and deploying LLM-powered systems, including retrieval-augmented generation (RAG) pipelines, agentic AI workflows, and intelligent automation. My work focuses on applying concepts in computer science and machine learning to optimize real-world systems, with measurable impact on performance, reliability, safety, and cost. I bring a strong engineering mindset shaped by experience in large-scale automation, testing, and system optimization, complemented by a solid foundation in reinforcement learning and optimization. I enjoy turning advanced AI ideas into practical, production-ready solutions that reduce manual effort and scale effectively.
